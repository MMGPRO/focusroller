According to Peter Shalulile, "Mamelodi Sundowns can win five trophies." after winning the MTN8 semifinal

published: 24 September 2023 Sunday by MMG

Mamelodi Sundowns want to keep playing an excellent brand of football and winning championships, according to striker Peter Shalulile.

After defeating Kaizer Chiefs 3-2 on aggregate over the course of the two-legged semifinal yesterday at the Lucas Moripe Stadium in Atteridgeville, the Brazilians became the first team to advance to the MTN8 final.

Shalulile scored twice as Sundowns defeated Chiefs 2-1 in the home leg. Ashley du Preez scored for Sundowns.
Shalulile scored 10 seconds into the match before canceling out Du Preez's* penalty just before halftime to put an end to the contest.

In the MTN8 semifinal, Jose' Riveiro wants Orlando Pirates to "offer a good fight" against Stellenbosch FC.

Pirates will enter the MTN8 semi-final second leg against Stellenbosch FC with the upper hand after winning the first leg 2-1 at the Athlone Stadium. The match will take place at Orlando Stadium tonight at 3 p.m.
