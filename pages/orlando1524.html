In the MTN8 semifinal, Jose' Riveiro wants Orlando Pirates to "offer a good fight" against Stellenbosch FC.

published: 24 September 2023 Sunday by MMG

Pirates will enter the MTN8 semi-final second leg against Stellenbosch FC with the upper hand after winning the first leg 2-1 at the Athlone Stadium. The match will take place at Orlando Stadium tonight at 3 p.m.

Even though the Sea Robbers are favorites to go to their third straight Cup final, they won't stop short of anything. After losing consecutive games against Jwaneng Galaxy and Mamelodi Sundowns in the CAF Champions League and Dstv Premiership, respectively, Pirates are in desperate need of a victory.

Riveiro still doesn't anticipate complacency from his team, despite Stellies' recent struggles, which included three red cards in five straight defeats.
